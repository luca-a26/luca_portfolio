ABOUT ME
I am an MSc Environmental Consultancy and BA Geography graduate from the University of Southampton with a broad skillset covering data collection, spatial analysis, and report building. During my time at Mcgregor Coxall, I was able to develop my GIS and spatial analysis skills and play a crucial part in several projects. My work provided my team with a detailed and data-driven insight into project locations, enabling them to make more impactful decisions and deliver more benefits for local people and stakeholders.
My desire to build a career with GIS and spatial analysis started during my Masters research project, where I focused on using a multi-criteria decision-making suitability analysis to determine the optimal location for tidal energy turbines in the Bristol Channel. In 2023 I started at Mcgregor Coxall, where I was able to utilise my skills in a range of projects covering the environment, urban landscapes, resilience models and much more. As the only GIS expert at the UK side of the company, I set the standard for quality context maps, spatial analysis, and automation. I am excited to continue my development within the industry, exploring the latest developments in spatial analysis to expand my toolset and make a meaningful impact on the world around me.
